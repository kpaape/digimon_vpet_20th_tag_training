Digimon Vpet 20th: Tag Training

Info

  1. Select your first move by clicking A or B under "Player:" (the selected move does not matter as long as it is the move you made). From here on, the "Player:" buttons will display what move you should make next!
  2. Under "Enemy:", click on the move the enemy made. You will notice the list of the enemy's possible moves start to be eliminated.
  3. A move under "Player:" will now be highlighted, play that move.
  4. Repeat steps 2 & 3 until you win the match. If you selected an incorrect enemy move, click "Reset" and start over.
  5. After winning the match, click "Next" and the next set of enemy moves will be highlighted.
  6. Play the opposite attacks as the ones displayed under "Enemy's possible moves".
  7. Repeat steps 5 & 6.
